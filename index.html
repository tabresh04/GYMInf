Nutrition

Macro-nutrients

    Protein:

  • Role: Essential for muscle repair and growth.
  • Sources: Chicken, fish, eggs, tofu, legumes, protein shakes.
  • Recommended Intake: ~1.2 to 2.0 grams per kg of body weight for active individuals.

    Carbohydrates:

  • Role: Primary source of energy, especially during workouts.
  • Sources: Whole grains, vegetables, fruits, sweet potatoes, oats.
  • Recommended Intake: 3-5 grams per kg of body weight for moderate exercise.

    Fats:

  • Role: Supports hormone production and provides long-term energy.
  • Sources: Avocados, nuts, seeds, olive oil, fatty fish.
  • Recommended Intake: 20-35% of total daily calories.
Pre-Workout Nutrition

    Protein:

  • To preserve muscle mass (e.g., lean chicken, Greek yogurt).

    Carbohydrates:

  • Quick energy (e.g., fruits, oatmeal).

    Fats:

  • Timing: Eat 30 minutes to 2 hours before the workout.
  • Banana with peanut butter.
  • Oatmeal with berries and a scoop of protein powder.
Post-Workout Nutrition

    Protein:

  • To repair and rebuild muscles (e.g., whey protein, eggs).

    Carbohydrates:

  • To replenish glycogen stores (e.g., rice, potatoes).

    Fats:

  • Timing: Eat within 30-60 minutes post-exercise.
  • Grilled chicken with quinoa and vegetables.
  • Protein shake with a banana.
